About our program


Tradition since 1993

Enoch Recovery DDP Center is dedicated to providing comprehensive therapy to individuals facing alcohol and drug addictions. We focus on instilling a positive belief system that incorporates morals, values, and principles, aiming to develop men and women of integrity. The facilities at Enoch Recovery DDP Center are safe, comfortable, and have a beautiful ambiance. Moreover, Enoch Recovery DDP Center emphasizes education rather than punishment for those who choose to drink and drive. You can trust that you will be treated with the utmost kindness, courtesy, and respect during your time at the center. We believe in the potential for growth and second chances in life, understanding that people can make mistakes and deserve respect and support on their journey towards recovery
