Welcome to Enoch Recovery DDP Center


Our Impaired Driver Program (IDP), also known as the Drinking Driver Program (DDP), is dedicated to helping individuals on their journey to recovery from alcohol and drug-related issues. We provide comprehensive therapy and support to empower you to make positive changes and build a better future.

To participate in our IDP program, you'll need to follow these steps:

  • In order to qualify for the IDP, also known as the Drinking Driver Program (DDP), you need a DWI conviction. After your conviction, you'll receive a letter from DMV indicating your eligibility for the program.
  • Visit your local DMV office and pay a $75 enrollment fee. Be sure to inform them that you'll be attending Enoch Recovery DDP Center.
  • DMV will provide you with an instruction sheet about our school and payment process, along with our schedule.
  • The IDP program costs $233 and consists of seven weekly sessions each lasting two hours
